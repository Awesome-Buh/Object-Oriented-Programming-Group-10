Who We Are

The Smarter Way to Travel Cameroon

GoFast is Cameroon's first fully digital inter-city bus ticketing platform. We've taken the hassle out of travel — no queues, no last-minute scrambles, no overpriced roadside touts.

From the bustling streets of Douala to the cool highlands of Bamenda, GoFast gets you there on time, every time. Our platform partners with the country's most trusted transport agencies to bring you a single, seamless booking experience.

Whether you're a student heading home, a businessperson on deadline, or a family on holiday — GoFast was built for you.
